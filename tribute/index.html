Pauline Oliveros

Composer, accordianist, electronic pioneer, listener.

"Take a walk at night. Walk so silently that the bottoms of your feet become ears."

Pauline Oliveros, Sonic Meditations V: Native

A Rough Timeline

  • 1932 - Born, Houston, Texas
  • 1941 - Begins to play accordion
  • 1952 - Arrives in California with an accordion and $300
  • 1953 - Aquires her first tape recorder
  • 1966 - Becomes Director for the Mills Tape Music Center
  • 1967 - Leaves Mills to start electronic music studio at University of California, San Diego
  • 1971 on - Begins and develops her "Sonic Mediataions," turning away from traditional composition
  • 1976-79 - Serves as Director of the Center for Music Experiment at UCSD
  • 1981 - Leaves the University and relocates to upstate New York
  • 1988 - Coins the term "Deep Listening" and forms the Deep Listening Band with Stuart Dempster and David Gamper
  • 2016 - dies at age 84
